what owe does

the details, handled.

auto-extraction.

owe reads new messages and finds commitments automatically. who said what, who owes what, with the original quote.

auto-resolution.

when a commitment is fulfilled in the conversation — a file shared, a task confirmed — owe marks it done. no manual work.

follow-ups.

things they owe you with no reply. owe surfaces them, drafts a polite nudge, and lets you send it in one tap.

bot commands.

message yourself on WhatsApp to check commitments. "owe @Alex" shows what's open. "commitments" lists everything.

on privacy

your chats, your machine.

stays on your machine

owe connects directly to WhatsApp's servers using the same protocol as WhatsApp Web. messages are decrypted and processed locally. nothing is stored on a cloud server.

leaves your machine

only the small context needed for extraction — sent to Claude (by Anthropic) for commitment analysis, then forgotten. no chat logs. no training. no human review.

disconnect WhatsApp in settings. delete the app, and every trace goes with it.
